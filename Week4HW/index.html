Not your average badger

Badgers live in North America, Europe, and Asia, but honey badgers are native to Africa, Middle East, and India.

While badgers are omnivorous, honey badgers are carnivorous. They are not picky eaters. This little badger eats much bigger animals, such as crocodiles, and cobras.

Facts about them

  • They can spray like skunks
  • They can bite through a tortoise shell
  • They're in the Guinness World Records as "World's Most Fearless Creature"

Characteristics

  • Weight: Male: 20.7 lbs, Female: 13.7 lbs
  • Length: Male: 2.4 ft, Female: 2.1 ft
